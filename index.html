Our Story

The Vault Opens
When It Opens.

VibeVault started with a question: What if we created something truly scarce? Not artificial scarcity. Real scarcity. Only 1750 keys will ever exist in the foundation series.

Each key is locally fabricated in Johannesburg from solid 304 stainless steel. Individually laser-engraved with unique IDs. Permanently archived in our registry. Once all 1750 are claimed, the foundation series closes forever.

But this isn't just a one-time purchase. Your key is a lifetime membership. Quarterly drops, exclusive events, and a community of 1750 like-minded individuals. The Vault isn't just a product—it's a system designed for longevity.

Collect all three foundation keys and you become a Vault Master. On January 2027, the Grand Vault opens. Inside? An exclusive black-tie event, the accumulated prize pool, and the company of the other Vault Masters who saw the vision.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

VibeVault respects your privacy. We collect only essential data: name, contact details, shipping addresses, and order history.

Key Registry: Your key ownership is recorded in our permanent blockchain-backed registry. This data is public only if you opt-in to the Founders Wall.

Marketing: We email quarterly drop announcements only. No spam. Unsubscribe anytime in account settings.

Data Security: All payments processed via PayFast (PCI-DSS compliant). We never store card details.

Terms of Service

Pre-Orders: Deposits (R150-R400) secure your key and serial number. Balance due 2 weeks before shipping. Deposits refundable until fabrication begins.

Key Ownership: Keys are collectible items granting membership access. Not financial instruments. Resale allowed but warranty voids.

Quarterly Drops: Available exclusively to key holders. Limited quantities. First-come-first-served with tier-based early access.

Grand Vault Event: January 2027. Attendance requires Vault Master status (all 3 foundation keys). Prize pool distributed as described.

Shipping: South Africa only. R0 shipping for Gold tier. R99 flat rate for others. Local pickup available in JHB/CT.
